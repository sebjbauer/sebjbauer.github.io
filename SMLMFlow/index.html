STORM (Stochastic Optical Reconstruction Microscopy)

STORM achieves super-resolution by controlling the fluorescence of individual molecules, allowing precise localization of single fluorophores with nanometer accuracy.

PALM (Photoactivated Localization Microscopy)

PALM uses photoactivatable fluorescent proteins to achieve single-molecule localization, enabling detailed visualization of protein distributions in living cells.
